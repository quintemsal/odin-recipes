Ingredients:

  • 6 tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (7.75 oz) can of "El Pato" sauce
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 3 carrots, peeled and cubed
  • chicken broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 tbs. cumin
  • 1/2 cup water
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Bring 6 whole tomatoes to a boil in a small pot.
  2. Transfer to a blender: 3/4 cup water, onion halves, and whole garlic clove. Blend until smooth.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chopped onion and serrano pepper. Cook until onion is transparent.
  4. Add minced garlic; cook until fragrant (about 1 minute)
  5. Add ground beef; cook until browned (about 8 minutes)
  6. Pour canned tomato sauce into the beef mixture. Cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Mix in potatoes and carrots. Cook for 2 minutes
  8. Add chicken broth, bay leaf and cumin.
  9. Season with salt and pepper.
  10. Mix well, reduce heat, and simmer until most of the liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
