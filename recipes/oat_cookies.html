Ingredients:

  • 1 cup wheat/all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon bourbon vanilla extract
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 and 1/2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 3/4 cup sweetened dried cranberries
  • 3/4 cup white chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl beat the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. Mix in the egg and then the vanilla, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed.
  4. Slowly add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Add the oats, dried cranberries, and white chocolate chips. Mix until fully combined.
  6. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  8. Take small scoops of cookie dough and roll them into small balls.
  9. Gently press down with your hand to flatten each ball of cookie dough slightly
  10. Bake in separate batches at 350°F for 10-13 minutes.
  11. Allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5-10 minutes.
